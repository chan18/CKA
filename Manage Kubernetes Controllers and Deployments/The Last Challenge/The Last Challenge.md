# The Last Challenge

**Welcome to the Final Challenge!**

This is your last chance to experiment in the environment. Clicking **Finish Lab** will end this little world that flittered into existence just for you.

Take this opportunity to try new things. Don't be afraid to break anything; be curious!

**Here are some things to try out:**

- Identify the RolloutStrategy configured in the **coredns** deployment in the **kube-system** namespace.
- Create a CronJob that removes all files under **/tmp** every 10 mins using the **busybox:1.36** image. You have unlimited power within this virtual world—take the time you need for unguided learning.
